Participate in Learnathon 2.0?

Learnathon invites the participation under the following four categories.
CATEGORY 1

State/ Union Territories/ Parastatal bodies/ Urban Local Bodies/ Smart City SPVs/ Any other Administrative Body

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Submit a solution implemented by your state or city that has benefited the department, citizens, or addressed any urban challenge.

Rewards & Recognition
POPULAR CHOICE FOR CATEGORY-1
Evaluation by panel of experts
Criteria: Innovation, Impact, Replicability, Sustainability
Public Voting post initial assessment
Entry with highest vote with final decision of committee will be the winner
Win cash prizes upto 6,00,000 and get certified by the JS, MoHUA and Director, NIUA
CATEGORY 2

Industry

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Develop or present a solution that has been implemented or is implementable in an Indian city, demonstrating clear benefits to citizens or addressing a specific urban challenge.

CATEGORY 3

Academia

PROBLEM STATEMENT 1:
Create a learning resource or e-learning module on one of the 10 themes provided, designed for ULB (Urban Local Body) officials. The resource should facilitate a deeper understanding of the selected topic or theme.
PROBLEM STATEMENT 2:
Present an implemented or implementable urban solution that addresses one of the 10 themes, focusing on one of the three aspects: Capacity Building, Adoption of Technology, Strategy and Planning
